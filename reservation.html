Personal Information

We follow time in 12-hour format

Our Offerings

  • We offer a unique western food on our menu. Kindly indicate your dietary restriction (if any) and /or any special occasion arrangements(if needed) upon making the reservation with 24 hours minimum prior to your dine-in date

Reservation Policy

  • Cancellation & Changes:

  • Please let us know at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or change your reservation
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of your reservation will result in a charge of RM60 per diner
  • We accept reservation between 10am - 7pm for us to prepare each customer their dining experience

  • Table Hold Time:

  • We can hold your table for up to 20 minutes past your reservation time
  • After 20 minutes, your table may be released to other guests to avoid delays for the next seating

Cancellation & Modification Policy

  • Notice Period:

  • Changes to reservation times or cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to the reservation
  • After the 24-hour mark, no changes or cancellations are allowed

  • Late Cancellations:

  • Cancellation made within 24 hours of the reservation constitutes as a late cancellation and may be subject to a charge of RM150 nett per diner.
  • No-show reservations will also be charged RM100 nett per diner.
