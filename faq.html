FAQs

How do I place an order on the website ?

From the website's home page, you can browse the menu and select your favourite items. Once selected, click the "Add to Cart" button and follow the page prompts to complete the order process.

I can't log into my account, what should I do ?

If you are unable to log in to your account, please first ensure that the username and password you entered are correct. If you forget your password, you can click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the prompts to reset your password. If the problem persists, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Can I change my username ?

Normally, you may not be able to change your username directly. If you wish to change your username, please contact our customer service team with your account details, and our team will do their best to accommodate your needs.

How do I pay for my order ?

We support a variety of payment methods, including credit card, Alipay and WeChat Pay. You can choose your preferred payment method on the checkout page and follow the system prompts to complete the payment process.

Can I log in on multiple devices simultaneously ?

Typically, you can log into your account on multiple devices at the same time. However, for account security reasons, we recommend that you log in on a personal device, protect your personal information on public devices, and avoid staying logged in for a long time on public devices.

Can I modify my order ?

Once an order is submitted, you generally will not be able to modify it directly. However, you can try to make changes by contacting the customer service team, provided that the order has not yet been prepared.
